AIDU Color Treatment

How should coral, teal, and violet be used in the hero and primary UI?

A

Atmospheric Glow

Colors as ambient light sources. Aurora bands, depth fog, subtle glow halos. Text and UI elements stay white/light gray against the dark canvas.

  • • Maximum readability
  • • Colors feel like natural light
  • • Best for shader-heavy scenes
  • • More subtle, premium feel
B

Gradient Harmony

Colors blend into rich multi-stop gradients. Used in backgrounds, card fills, buttons, and dividers. High visual impact and energy.

  • • Bold, energetic feel
  • • More "typical AI startup"
  • • Can reduce text contrast
  • • Works well on cards/CTAs
C

Precision Accents

Colors as sharp, intentional signals. Borders, icon fills, active states, hover glows, and progress indicators. Clean and functional.

  • • Most legible and accessible
  • • Clean, tool-like feel
  • • Colors feel more "expensive"
  • • Good for dashboard/UI

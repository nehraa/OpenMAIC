Why Cinematic Serif (A)?

Reasons

  • Academic credibility — Education brand needs intellectual weight. Serifs signal expertise.
  • Contrast with tech — The AI is cutting-edge; the typography feels timeless and trustworthy.
  • Brand differentiation — Most AI startups use bold sans. Serif = memorable.
  • Cinematic pairing — Works beautifully with the aurora shader's organic movement.

Considerations

  • Slightly lower legibility at small sizes — mitigated by large hero treatment.
  • More "serious" tone — but that's appropriate for education/institutions.
A

Cinematic Serif — RECOMMENDED

Source Serif 4 for headlines. Inter for body. The contrast creates hierarchy without needing gradients or effects.
